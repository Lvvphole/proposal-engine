## Proposal Engine — Pilot Program Agreement

### 1. Service Description

Service Provider will process Contractor's supplier quotes and generate contractor-ready proposals using AI-powered extraction and formatting technology. Each proposal is subject to Contractor's review and approval before delivery to any customer.

### 2. Pilot Terms

**2.1 Free Period.** During the 30-day pilot period, Service Provider will process up to [50] supplier quotes at no charge.

**2.2 Contractor Responsibilities.** Contractor agrees to:
- Forward supplier quotes (PDF, image, or email) to Service Provider within 24 hours of receipt
- Review and approve/reject generated proposals within 48 hours
- Report closed proposals within 7 days of customer acceptance
- Provide feedback on extraction accuracy and proposal quality

**2.3 Service Provider Responsibilities.** Service Provider agrees to:
- Process received quotes within 4 business hours
- Maintain extraction accuracy above 85% on structured documents
- Never send a proposal without Contractor's explicit approval
- Keep all Contractor and customer data confidential

### 3. Post-Pilot Pricing

If Contractor elects to continue after the pilot period:

**3.1 Performance Fee.** [8-10]% of gross revenue from jobs closed using proposals generated by the Service.

**3.2 Attribution.** A proposal is "generated by the Service" if it was produced through the extraction pipeline and approved by Contractor, regardless of subsequent modifications.

**3.3 Reporting and Payment.** Contractor reports closed proposals weekly via the tracking dashboard. Payment is due Net 15 from close date.

**3.4 No Monthly Minimum.** There is no monthly fee, subscription, or minimum commitment. Contractor pays only on closed revenue.

### 4. Data and Confidentiality

**4.1** All supplier quotes, customer information, and pricing data remain Contractor's property.

**4.2** Service Provider may use anonymized extraction data to improve system accuracy but will never share identifiable Contractor or customer information with third parties.

**4.3** Contractor may request deletion of all stored data at any time.

### 5. Termination

Either party may terminate this agreement with 7 days written notice. Upon termination, Service Provider will delete all stored Contractor data within 30 days.

### 6. Limitation of Liability

Service Provider's AI-generated proposals are tools for Contractor's review, not substitutes for professional judgment. Contractor is solely responsible for the accuracy and legality of all proposals delivered to customers.
